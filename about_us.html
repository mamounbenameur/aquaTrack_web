Gold Medalists - iFEST 2025

The AquaTrack Project

We were proud to be awarded the Gold Medal at iFEST 2025 with our project AquaTrack. The objective of this project is to provide solutions that ensure the conservation and preservation of water, a valuable resource for future generations.

For that, the plan is to build an application aligned to an IoT device that is capable of monitoring the flow rate of water continuously. The application will measure the actual and our projected utilization of water, based on our mathematical model, to evaluate and optimize the utilization of water in a better way.
